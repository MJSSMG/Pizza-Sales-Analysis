🍕 Pizza Sales Data Analysis
A Data Analytics case study analyzing pizza sales data to uncover business insights, identify revenue drivers, and build interactive dashboards for decision-making.
This project demonstrates how SQL and Power BI can transform raw transactional data into actionable insights for business growth.

📌 Project Overview
The pizza restaurant collects large volumes of transactional sales data but lacks clear insights into sales performance, product popularity, and customer purchasing behavior.
This project analyzes 48,620 pizza sales records to identify:
-Sales trends
-Customer preferences
-Best and worst performing pizzas
-Revenue drivers
The insights are presented through an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven decision making.

🎯 Business Objectives
The analysis aims to answer the following business questions:
-What is the total revenue generated?
-How many pizzas were sold?
-What is the average order value?
-Which pizza categories and sizes perform best?
-Which pizzas are the top and bottom sellers?
-What are the daily and monthly sales trends?

**📊 Power BI Dashboard**
Two interactive dashboards were created.

Sales Overview Dashboard
Displays:
-Total Revenue
-Average Order Value
-Total Orders
-Total Pizzas Sold
-Average Pizzas per Order
-Daily order trends
-Monthly sales trends
-Sales distribution by pizza category
-Sales distribution by pizza size

Best & Worst Sellers Dashboard
Displays:
-Top 5 pizzas by revenue
-Top 5 pizzas by quantity sold
-Top 5 pizzas by total orders
-Bottom 5 pizzas by revenue
-Bottom 5 pizzas by quantity sold
-Bottom 5 pizzas by total orders

💡 Key Insights
-Classic pizza category generates the highest sales.
-Large size pizzas contribute the most revenue.
-Thai Chicken Pizza generates the highest revenue.
-Classic Deluxe Pizza leads in quantity and total orders.
-Brie Carre Pizza performs the worst across revenue, quantity, and orders.
-Sales peak on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday.

📈 Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were made:

1️⃣ Promote Best-Selling Products
Highlight popular pizzas in promotions and advertisements.

2️⃣ Optimize the Menu
Improve or remove underperforming pizzas.

3️⃣ Improve Inventory Planning
Ensure availability of ingredients used in high-demand pizzas.

4️⃣ Optimize Staffing
Increase staff during peak sales periods.
